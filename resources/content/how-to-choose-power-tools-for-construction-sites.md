Not every high-spec power tool is right for daily site conditions. Use this practical framework:

## Duty cycle first
Pick tools based on expected daily workload, not brochure peak performance.

## Serviceability and spares
Availability of accessories and replaceable parts matters more than minor spec differences.

## Power source strategy
Align battery platforms where possible to reduce charger clutter and improve field uptime.

## Ergonomics and safety
Operator fatigue impacts productivity. Balance, grip, anti-vibration features, and guard quality are critical.
