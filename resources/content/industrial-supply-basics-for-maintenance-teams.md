Maintenance teams move faster when supply systems are standardized.

## Standardize critical consumables
Define approved items per asset class so teams can reorder without repeated approvals.

## Maintain a minimum stock level
Use min-max levels for fast-moving consumables and safety items.

## Keep a quality-first vendor list
Cheap substitutes can increase downtime through repeat failures.

## Review monthly failure patterns
Link supply data to equipment failures and adjust inventory toward preventive action.
